About Us

The Hindu Center at NYU is being organized by a group of students working under the auspices of the Hindu Student Council.

There are also several non-students involved, as the Hindu Center is a resource for the students at NYU, and the wider Hindu community.

Things are still in the initial stages of formation. We will provide contact info and other details soon. For now, contact us by showing up at the Center on Saturdays between 3-5pm.
