MATSYA Saves the world from a cosmic flood (Noah’s ark), with the help 

of the boat made of the Vedas (knowledge), Satyavrata devotee leads 2 of 

every kind out of flood.and all living beings. Hayagriva steals and tries to 

destroy the Vedas, but Matsya finds the demon, kills him, and returns the 

Vedas. 

VISHNU_PRESENTATION_sm-html.html

KURMA tortoise avatar. He supports mount Meru, while the gods and demons 

churn the cosmic ocean with the help of serpent Vasuki to produce the nectar of 

immortality (just like churning milk to produce butter). The churning produces 

both the good and the bad, including poison and immortality nectar. Nobody 

wants the poison, everyone wants the immortality nectar. The demons attempt 

to steal the nectar, wherein Vishnu appears as enchantress Mohini avatar, for 

whom they all fall, and give her the nectar.

VISHNU_PRESENTATION_sm-html.html

VARAHA Boar avatar. He rescues goddess earth when the demon 

Hiranyaksha kidnaps her and hides her into the depths of cosmic 

ocean. The boar finds her and kills the demon, and the goddess holds 

onto the tusk of the boar as he lifts her back to the surface.

VISHNU_PRESENTATION_sm-html.html

NARSIMHA Half lion-half man avatar. Demon king Hiranyakashipu becomes 

enormously powerful, gains special powers by which no man or animal could kill 

him, not in the day or night (twilight) not indoors or outdoors (courtyard/threshold) 

no weapon (barehands got ripped open) then bullies and persecutes people who

disagree with him, including his own son. The Man-Lion avatar creatively defeats 

those special powers, kills Hiranyakashipu, and rescues demon's son Prahalad 

who opposes his own father. 

VISHNU_PRESENTATION_sm-html.html

VAMANA Dwarf avatar. Demon king Bali Prahalad’s grandson gains enormous 

powers, ruling the entire universe and abusing it. The dwarf avatar approaches 

Bali in the form of a monk, when Bali is trying to show off by giving alms at a 

sacrifice. Bali offers the dwarf any riches he wants, the monk refuses and asks 

for three steps of land. Bali grants it to him. The dwarf grows, in his first step 

takes the earth, the second all of the heavens, and for the third the netherworld 

where Bali returns to receiving liberation. 

VISHNU_PRESENTATION_sm-html.html

PARASHURAMA Sage with an axe avatar. The warrior class gets too 

powerful, and seizes other people's property for their own pleasure. The 

avatar appears as a sage with an axe, kills the king Arjuna and all his 

warrior companions.

RAMA the beloved and noble king. Who won Sita’s hand by

stringing and breaking Lord Shiva’s bow with his might.

BUDDHA The enlighted one founder of Buddhism. Some Hindu texts replace 

Buddha with Balarama or with Rishabhanatha, the first Tīrthankara of Jainism.

KALKI The last avatar appears as man with a white horse with wings, the 

messiah and end of days apocalypse projected to end the Kali yuga, in order 

that the cosmos may renew and restart.
