Welcome Back!

The NYU Hindu Center reopens this Saturday Feb 10th

Sangeetha Kowsik will be leading an Interactive Ganesha Puja. All prayers are in Sanskrit, but we provide English translations so everyone can participate.

The puja will be in room 475, from 3 to 5 PM

Everyone will receive their own Ganesha, and all materials will be provided.

Dinner will be served afterwards.

We Hope to See You There!

Join Us!

Come join the Hindu Community at the first Hindu Center at New York University.

Community members are encouraged to come too. You don't have to be a student, or affiliated with NYU to attend. All are welcome.

Community involvement is important in order to support the students, and have continuity in the Center since students will come and go.

A short puja will be held Saturday afternoons from 3-5 PM as noted. Major holidays will be observed too.

Get involved so we can all benefit. This can be the beginning of a permanent Hindu Center at NYU for all to use. While there, sign our mailing list, and if you like, we will keep you informed of coming events at the center.

Note: Please bring photo ID

You will have to log in at the front desk. Please bring photo ID, and tell the guards you're here for the Hindu Center.
