There will be a Saraswati Puja this Saturday, 10 December, 2016, from 3 - 5 pm. Those of you facing exams should avail yourselves of the help of the Goddess.

Join Us!

Come join the Hindu Community at the first Hindu Center at New York University.

Community members are encouraged to come too. You don't have to be a student, or affiliated with NYU to attend.

Community involvement is important in order to support the students, and have continuity in the Center since students will come and go.

A short puja will be held every Saturday afternoon from 3-5 PM. Major holidays will be observed too.

Get involved so we can all benefit. This can be the beginning of a permanent Hindu Center at NYU for all to use. While there, sign our mailing list, and if you like, we will keep you informed of coming events at the center.

Note: Please bring photo ID

You will have to log in at the front desk. Please bring some photo ID, and tell the guards you're here for the Hindu Center.
